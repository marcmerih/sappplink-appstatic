Mission

Sapplink’s primary mission is to create breakthroughs in product design, and develop web / mobile based applications with complex business logic, employing novel technologies & operations research methods to create end-to-end solutions that provide users with unparalleled experiences whilst generating value to all stakeholders.

Sapplink’s secondary mission is to sustain and grow its Pledge to Nature, and become a large Canadian contributor to wildlife preservation, ecosystem boosting, and oxygenation through tree planting, mainly Sequoias.


Vision

Sappplink’s vision is to become a notorious name in the software industry, for its unique design style, and cutting edge projects.


Values

Trust: Mutual trust and honesty between all stakeholders involved in the delivery of a project, to go above & beyond what initial promises.

Passion: A dedication towards designing and developing the best application.

Fairness: Promotion of respect & a level playing field for all stakeholders.

Accountability: Taking responsibility of, and liability towards the completed work.


Behind the Name

The word Sapplink was originated from the English word ‘Sapling’, which is the word used to describe a young tree. The clever transformation of the word portrays a cultural devotion towards nature, while depicting its focus on technology.


The Logo

Sapplink’s logo is yet another portrayal of the devotion to its Pledge to Nature, as it depicts a singular leaf of a Sequoia tree - one of the largest living things on the planet.
